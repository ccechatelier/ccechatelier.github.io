    About Me

    My Intro

    My research focuses on the catalytic properties of metallic and intermetallic (nano)materials for reduction and oxidation reactions, using a combination of experimental and theoretical approaches, such as Surface X-ray Diffraction, Bragg Coherent X-ray Diffraction Imaging, Molecular Statics, Molecular Dynamics, and Density Functional Theory
